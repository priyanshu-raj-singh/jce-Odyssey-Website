Technical Events

Smriti Sangram (Memory Battle)

Sharpen Your Vision,Strengthen Your Recall!

  • Entry Fees: Rs.75/-
    Team Size: 1
  •  Round 1 (Observation) - 2 minutes Display Setup: Various civil engineering tools, materials, and accessories will be arranged.
  • Observation Time: Participants get 2 minutes to observe and memorize the items.
  • Restrictions: No touching, note-taking, mobile phones, or discussions allowed.
  • Positioning: Participants must stand at a designated distance; moving closer leads to disqualification.
  • Post-Observation: After 2 minutes, participants leave the area and proceed to the next round for memory-based evaluation.
  •  Round 2: (Recall & Write) (5 Minutes) Task: Write down the names of all remembered items. Time Limit: 5 minutes
  • Accuracy: Only correct technical names count; minor spelling errors allowed unless unrecognizable.
